AI-powered daily streaming recommendations for Yahoo Fantasy Baseball. Analyzes your matchup, scores every available player, and emails you the best moves every morning.

Every morning, the bot pulls data from Yahoo Fantasy, MLB, FanGraphs, Baseball Savant, and PitcherList. It scores available players against your current H2H matchup needs, assesses risk, and sends everything through Claude for final analysis. You get a ranked list of streams with drop candidates and FAAB bids.

Features

⚾

Pitcher Streaming

Probable pitcher matchups scored by K upside, QS probability, ERA/WHIP risk, and park factors.

📈

Matchup-Aware

Reads your live H2H category scores and targets the categories you need to win this week.

🤖

AI Reasoning

Claude synthesizes stats, context, and strategy into actionable recommendations with reasoning.

💵

FAAB Bids

Budget-aware bid calculator that scales with time-of-season, competition, and player value.

📊

Drop Ranker

Protects your core roster and identifies the safest drops based on ownership, performance, and role.

🎓

Self-Improving

Tracks every recommendation outcome and tunes scoring weights weekly based on prediction accuracy.
