A Small Pause

A small pause between you and the app you chose to slow down on.

What it does

A Small Pause places a brief, intentional moment between you and an app you have chosen to slow down on. Open the app, and before it appears, A Small Pause fades in with four slow breath cycles or a single quiet question. Then you continue, or you close the app and do something else. Either choice is fine.

The pause is short by design. Most interventions finish in under thirty seconds, and the skip button is visible from the first frame. There is no "are you sure" confirmation, no countdown lock, no streak to break, no guilt for skipping.

Common questions

Why does A Small Pause on iPhone need the Shortcuts app?
iOS does not let a normal app detect when another app opens. The Shortcuts app does. During onboarding you create a one-time automation that opens A Small Pause whenever your chosen app opens. It runs entirely on your phone. I do not receive the trigger and am not notified when it fires.

Why does A Small Pause on Android need Usage Access and the "Display over other apps" permission?
Android needs Usage Access to tell A Small Pause that one of your chosen apps has come to the foreground. The "Display over other apps" permission is used solely to bring A Small Pause to the front in response; A Small Pause does not actually draw any overlay on top of other apps. Data never leaves your device.

Does A Small Pause track me?
No. There are no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, no server of mine that you talk to. Your chosen apps, your pause type, your reflection prompts — all of it lives only on your phone. The only exception is the optional $1.99 unlock purchase, which is processed by Apple or Google and by RevenueCat. See the privacy policy for details.

I skipped ten times in a row. Is that bad?
No. Skipping is a feature, not a failure. It is what makes reflecting, when you choose to reflect, mean something.

How do I delete all my data?
Uninstall the app, or go to Settings inside A Small Pause and tap "Reset all data." Because nothing leaves your phone, there is nothing for me to delete on my end.
