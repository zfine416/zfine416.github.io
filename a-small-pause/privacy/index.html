Privacy Policy

Effective: April 11, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 11, 2026

Summary

A Small Pause is built to leave you alone. Everything it does — the pause, the breathing exercise, the list of apps you want to slow down on — happens on your phone and stays on your phone. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no servers of mine that you talk to.

The only exception is the one-time unlock purchase, which is processed by your App Store or Google Play account and by the payment provider, RevenueCat. Details below.

Who I am

A Small Pause is made by Zachary Fine ("I", "me", "my"). You can contact me at support@zachary-fine.com.

What I collect

I do not collect any personal information from you in the app.

Specifically:

  • I do not create an account for you.
  • I do not know who you are.
  • I do not collect your email, phone number, name, or device identifier.
  • I do not track which apps you open, how long you spend in them, or whether you completed a breathing exercise.
  • I do not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising identifiers, or behavioral tracking of any kind.
  • I do not sell, share, or trade any data with anyone for any purpose.

Everything you do inside A Small Pause — your chosen apps, your pause type, your reflection prompts — is stored only in local storage on your device. If you uninstall the app, that data is deleted by your operating system.

Permissions

A Small Pause asks for different permissions on iOS and Android. In both cases, the permissions are used only for the core function of the app, and no data is transmitted off your device.

iOS

  • Shortcuts automation (user-configured). A Small Pause does not request any Apple Family Controls or Screen Time API entitlements. Instead, you are asked during onboarding to create a Shortcuts automation that opens A Small Pause when a chosen app opens. This automation runs entirely on your device. I do not receive the trigger, I am not notified when it fires, and I have no access to the Shortcuts app or its configuration.

Android

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS). Used to detect when a chosen app comes to the foreground, so that A Small Pause can surface the pause before you continue. I only check which app is currently in the foreground; I do not read, store, or transmit usage history of any kind. This data never leaves your device.
  • Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE). Used to keep a small background watcher running so the detection can happen while the app is not open. The watcher stops automatically when you have no apps on your pause list or when you revoke Usage Access.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS). Used only to show the ongoing watcher notification required by Android's foreground service rules, and to surface the pause as a full-screen notification on locked devices. I do not send marketing or promotional notifications.
  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW). Used solely to bring A Small Pause to the foreground when you open a chosen app. A Small Pause does not draw any overlay views over other apps. The permission is only used for its documented ability to launch an activity from the background on an unlocked device.
  • Full-screen intent (USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT). Used as a fallback path to surface the pause on some devices and on the lock screen.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time via your phone's Settings. If you revoke a permission required for the Android watcher, the watcher stops and the pause will no longer appear until you re-grant the permission.

Purchases

A Small Pause offers a one-time in-app purchase that unlocks support for more than one paused app. This purchase is processed by:

  1. Your App Store (Apple) or Google Play (Google) account. Apple or Google handles the transaction, charges you, and delivers the receipt. Their respective privacy policies apply to the payment itself.
  2. RevenueCat. I use RevenueCat to verify your purchase and, if you reinstall the app or move to a new device, to restore it. RevenueCat receives a random anonymous identifier generated on your device, along with the Apple or Google receipt. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any other personal information from A Small Pause. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on how they handle purchase data.

I do not see or store your payment information. I do not know who made a purchase.

Children

A Small Pause is a tool for adults who have chosen to add friction to their own phone. It is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. If you are under the age of majority in your country, please do not install or use A Small Pause without a parent or guardian's supervision.

Security

Because I do not collect or transmit your data, there is no server of mine that could be breached. The only data about your use of A Small Pause is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, biometrics, full-disk encryption). I encourage you to keep your device's operating system up to date.

Your rights

Because I do not collect personal information from you, there is no personal information for me to correct, export, or delete. If you want to delete all A Small Pause data from your device, uninstall the app or use the "Reset all data" option in Settings.

If you made a purchase, the record of that purchase is held by Apple or Google and by RevenueCat. You can request deletion of your RevenueCat record by contacting me at support@zachary-fine.com.

Changes to this policy

If I change this policy in a way that affects what data is collected or how it is handled, I will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify users through the app's update notes. For material changes I will make reasonable efforts to give notice before the change takes effect.
