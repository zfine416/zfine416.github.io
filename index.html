About Me

Hi!! My name is Zachary Fine. I am a Full-Stack Web Developer residing in Brooklyn, NY. As a developer and a problem solver, I'm passionate about what I do and I believe that anything is possible. I grew up in a suburb just outside of New York City. I have an older sister and a younger, both of whom I am very close with and yes I am very much a middle child!! I had always excelled in Math and was fascinated with computers, so in High School I decided to teach myself how to code. Since then I have dabbled with several different languages, some most people have never even heard of. I love a good coding challenge, on codewars my current score is a 750... no big deal. In the past, I have taught people with absolutely no coding experience how to make web applications at both General Assembly and Codecademy. While this was extremely gratifying, it also made me a much stronger developer. While my main trade is a Web Developer, I am also extremely passionate about Design. Aside from writing code, I played college hockey at Indiana University, I am an avid guitar player and a huge dog person, this my dog Kelso's Instagram.
